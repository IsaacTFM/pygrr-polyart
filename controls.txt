How to use PolyArt?

~ left-click on a point and drag to move the point
~ right-click on a point to remove it
~ left-click on a line to create a new point
~ click on the buttons to interact
~ type and hit enter on an entry to submit
~ click the "colormixer" png to open the colormixer
~ use WSAD or UPDOWNLEFTRIGHT to move the model
~ use Q and E to rotate the model
